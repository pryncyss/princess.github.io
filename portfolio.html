DJBomens Boarding House(Payment System)

This project focuses on developing a payment system for a boarding house, providing a convenient and efficient payment solution for both the boarding house management and the tenants. The boarding house offers accommodation services to individuals seeking affordable and comfortable living arrangements.

Client-Server Program and RMI Implementation

Server-side features include Database functionalities using MySQL, performing Save/Store, Fetch, Delete, Sort, and Update Data. In the Client-side, an XML to be extracted must include a series (5 or more) of students with their names, age, addresses, and contact numbers.
